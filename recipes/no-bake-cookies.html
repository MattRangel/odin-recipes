No Bake Cookies

Description

Easily made peanut butter chocolate cookies. No oven required! Takes only 10 min of work.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups sugar
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 4 tbsp unsalted butter
  • 1/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 3 cups rolled oats
  • 1 cup smooth peanut butter
  • 1 tbsp vanilla extract
  • Large pinch salt

Steps

  1. Line baking sheet with wax paper.
  2. Put the milk, sugar, butter, and cocoa in a medium pot.
  3. Put the pot to boil on medium heat, string occasionally.
  4. Once the pot begins to boil, wait one minute and then remove from heat.
  5. Mix in the oatmeal, peanut butter, vanilla, and salt.
  6. Put small spoonfulls of the mixture on the wax paper.
  7. Leave to dry for 30 minutes, or until fully dry.
